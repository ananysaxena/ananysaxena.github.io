Creative Perspective

My passion lies in crafting highly detailed props, stylized and realistic characters, and immersive architectural spaces. I approach every mesh with a strong sense of composition, color theory, and visual storytelling—ensuring that every digital sculpt carries organic depth and emotional weight.

Bridging professional DCC suites (Blender, ZBrush, Maya) with industry-leading lookdev toolkits, I focus on delivering clean topology, optimal UV mapping, and high-fidelity PBR texturing. With over 3 years of 3D modeling and lighting exploration (including 1.8+ years of dedicated studio experience), I strive to create assets that are as technically efficient as they are visually arresting.
